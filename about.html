About Us

Welcome to UPMIP Subsidy Calculator, an online platform developed to simplify subsidy calculations for various micro irrigation systems under the Uttar Pradesh Micro Irrigation Project (UPMIP).

This website has been developed by Er. Kaushal Shakya, an Agricultural Engineer with practical experience in the field of micro irrigation systems, including Drip Irrigation, Mini Sprinkler, Portable Sprinkler and other water-efficient technologies.

Our objective is to provide farmers, dealers, field engineers and agricultural professionals with a reliable and easy-to-use calculator for estimating unit cost, subsidy amount and farmer contribution under different irrigation systems.

The platform is continuously being improved with new features and additional irrigation systems to make subsidy calculations more accurate and user-friendly.

We are committed to supporting sustainable agriculture and promoting efficient water management through modern irrigation technologies.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, simple and accessible digital tools that help farmers and agricultural professionals make informed decisions regarding micro irrigation systems and government subsidy schemes.
